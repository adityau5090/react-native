# Account Deletion Request Users can request the deletion of their account and associated data by sending an email to: [your-email@example.com](mailto:your-email@example.com) Please include the email address associated with your account in the request. Data deleted: * Profile information * User-generated content * App preferences Data retained: * Some information may be retained for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes for up to 30 days. Once the request is received, the account and associated data will be permanently deleted within 7 business days.
